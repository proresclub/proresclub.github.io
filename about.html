Pro Res

ProRes is the club where students can gain skills by doing projects and Our Aim is to convert Reasearch Papers into a Product.

OBJECTIVES AND MISSION

  • Developing product based research culture among students of Reva university.
  • Bridging the gap between theoretical and reality by encouraging research culture among students.
  • Embracing the product culture and building great startups from it
  • Creating a competitive spirit among students by organising Idea Hackathon Competition
  • Bringing industry icons and leaders to Reva University and organising Talks with great minds to introduce the reality to industry to students
  • Encouraging students to take up innovative and engineering challenges to solve real world problems.
