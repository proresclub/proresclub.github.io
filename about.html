Pro Res

ProRes is the club where students can gain skills by doing projects and our aim is to convert research papers into products.

OBJECTIVES AND MISSION

  • Developing a product-based research culture among students of Reva University.
  • Bridging the gap between theory and reality by encouraging research culture among students.
  • Embracing product culture and building great startups from it.
  • Creating a competitive spirit among students by organizing Idea Hackathon Competitions.
  • Bringing industry icons and leaders to Reva University and organizing talks with great minds.
  • Encouraging students to take up innovative challenges to solve real-world problems.
